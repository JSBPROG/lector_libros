He closed his eyes the vision formed instantly, and he straightened up terrified but enjoying at the same time the knowledge that now he was awake, that the vigil was protecting him, that soon there would be dawn, with the good deep sleep that is held at that hour, without images, without anything... He tried to keep his eyes open, the dream was stronger than he knew. He made a last effort, with his healthy hand he made a gesture towards the bottle of water; he did not manage to take it, his fingers closed in a vacuum again black, and the passage continued interminable, rock after rock, with sudden flashes of red, and the other mouth closed up again because the techo was going to end, sinking, a mouth covering the shadow of the red and the other mouth closed up again because the moon was shrinking and the face of the moon was getting closer to the sky
